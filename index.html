Invoice photo → spreadsheet → reports.

Task and reporting automation

The bot assigns tasks and collects reports.

>

A manager fills out a Google Sheet once a day with tasks for each trainee or employee. The bot sends tasks at the right time, reminds users if they don’t respond, and collects end-of-day reports. Reports can be dictated by voice and are automatically saved into a separate spreadsheet. The manager opens one file and instantly sees who did what, how many tasks were completed, and where issues exist — without calls or endless chats.

Estimated price: from 400 €.

Corporate assistant for managers

A company knowledge base that answers employees.

>

A corporate assistant is an internal company bot where management stores all important information: regulations, instructions, templates, FAQs, client databases, and internal rules. Employees no longer distract managers with routine questions — they ask the assistant and get accurate answers from the knowledge base. Managers are contacted only when the bot doesn’t know the answer or when a non-standard decision is required. This saves hours of time every day and makes the team more independent and efficient.

Secretary bot

Spoken agreements are never lost.

>

The secretary bot records all agreements with clients: who, what was discussed, deadlines, and conditions. It accepts voice and text messages, extracts names, companies, phone numbers, topics, and short summaries, and stores everything in Google Sheets. On request — for example, “What do we have with John?” — the bot shows the full history of agreements. Every morning it sends reminders about planned tasks and promises so nothing gets forgotten.

Estimated price: from 500 €.
